Our Journey of Faith

From humble beginnings to a thriving community of believers

The Vision Begin

Rise Generation International Church was planted in 2017 G.C through the vision of Pastor Abenet Legesse and his Wife Prohetess Kidist Markos,who heard God's call to raise a generation that will seek God and carry His mission.

First Official Service

We held our first official Sunday service in 2017 a rented community center with 15 people in attendance. The vision was clear: to create a space where young people could encounter God and grow in their faith.

Ministry Expansion

Our congregation grew more in members, and God continued to grow the church into great spirtual family,reaching people with gospel and Empoweringthe next generation

Digital Transformation

During the global pandemic, we quickly adapted to online services and virtual community. This period strengthened our commitment to reaching people wherever they are, leading to our hybrid ministry model.

New Sanctuary

By the grace of God, and through the dedication of His people, the goverment has granted us land, and now we are free from rent. This make a significant step forward and give us permanet home to worship train and reach more lives with the Gospel

Rise Generation International Church Impact

Today, Rise Generation International Church serves over 500 members and has planted sister churches around Ethiopia subcities. We continue to grow, adapt, and impact lives for God's kingdom.

Ministry Partnership

Advancing the Kingdom of God through unified leadership, service, and discipleship

Purpose

Our Ministry Partnership exists to advance the Kingdom of God by equipping believers, empowering communities, and proclaiming the Gospel through unified leadership, service, and discipleship. Together, we aim to glorify Christ by integrating spiritual growth, prophetic guidance, community transformation, and effective communication.

Mission Alignment

This partnership is built upon our shared mission to:

  • Proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ with truth and compassion
  • Disciple believers and strengthen the Body of Christ
  • Serve communities holistically, addressing both spiritual and practical needs
  • Empower women and families through faith-based mentorship and leadership
  • Use media and technology to reach the nations with the message of hope

Every activity, program, and partnership aligns with biblical values, Christ-centered ministry, and the vision entrusted to the leadership team.

Partnership Guide

1
Initial Contact

Interested partners submit an expression of interest or contact the General Secretary.

2
Review & Alignment

Leadership team reviews alignment with mission, values, and spiritual goals.

3
Partnership Proposal

Drafted by the General Secretary, reviewed by the Senior Pastor and Founders.

4
Approval & Agreement

Signed by the leadership team; partnership officially launched.

5
Implementation

Ministries, projects, and outreach activities commence under spiritual and administrative oversight.

6
Monitoring & Fellowship

Regular review meetings and shared testimonies strengthen collaboration.

7
Renewal & Growth

Annual reflection and prayer for continued alignment and impact.
